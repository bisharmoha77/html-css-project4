About Golden Plate

Discover our passion for culinary excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2001 by Chef Maria Rodriguez, Golden Plate began as a small family restaurant with a big dream: to create an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the art of fine cuisine.

What started as a 20-seat establishment has grown into one of the city's most acclaimed restaurants, earning numerous awards and recognition from culinary critics worldwide.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the kitchen. We source our ingredients from local farms and sustainable suppliers, ensuring that every dish not only tastes exceptional but also supports our community and environment.

Meet Our Executive Chef

Chef Maria Rodriguez

Executive Chef & Owner

With over 25 years of culinary experience, Chef Maria has trained in some of the world's finest kitchens, including Le Cordon Bleu in Paris and working alongside Michelin-starred chefs in New York.

Her innovative approach to traditional cuisine has earned Golden Plate numerous accolades, including:

  1. James Beard Award Nominee (2019, 2021)
  2. Best Restaurant - City Magazine (2020, 2022)
  3. Five-Star Rating - Culinary Review (2018-2024)
  4. Sustainable Restaurant Award (2021)
